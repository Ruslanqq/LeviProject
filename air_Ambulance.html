GROUND AMBULANCE SERVICES

We are offering 24/7 around-the-clock ground/air ambulance transportation using a wide range of ‎licensed ground ambulance and air ambulance aircrafts, state of the art medical equipment and ‎experienced doctors, nurses, paramedics and other personnel to ensure safe transfer and ‎supervision. ‎

Services offered include:

  • ALS, BLS, Ground Ambulance
  • Emergency / Non-emergency patient transfers, to nursing homes, clinics, hospitals, hotels and airports
  • Public event medical care, including on-site first aid stations, dedicated ambulances, and paramedics
  • Patient transportation between airports and medical institutions
    (picking up patients from an airplane to facilities)
  • On-site clinic anywhere in the world

RESCUE HELICOPTER AMBULANCE SERVICE

Due to mountainous in Central Asia sometimes it is impossible to reach the patient with a ground ‎ambulance and the only solution is to use a rescue medical helicopter. We have established ‎relationships with emergency helicopters ready to assist us 24/7 with professional rescue teams ‎of doctors, nurses, and paramedics.‎

AIR AMBULANCE SERVICE
  • Selection of Air ambulance including medical team and state of the art medical equipment for emergency medical flights according to the patient medical condition and needs and the flying time involved
COMMERCIAL FLIGHTS& MEDICAL ESCORT SERVICES

  • • Pre-travel medical assessment by our doctors to determine the most appropriate method of transport.
  • • Airline tickets for the patient, medical team member, and travel companion (where applicable)
  • • Secure airline specific medical clearance and government documentation as necessary Priority boarding and seating arrangements
  • • Booking commercial flights that accommodate patient's conditions with a medical escort team accompanying the patient during the flight, Hospital discharge and re-admission at destination

  • • Full medical assessment and monitoring throughout the entire transport
