  • Public event medical care, including on-site first aid stations, dedicated ambulances, and paramedics

PRIVATE HELICOPTER AMBULANCE SERVICE

Due to mountainous terrain is Central Asia sometimes it is impossible to reach the patient with a ground ambulance and the only solution is to use a helicopter. We have established relationships with emergency helicopters ready to assist us 24/7 with professional rescue teams of doctors, nurses, and paramedics.

PRIVATE AIR AMBULANCE SERVICE

Services offered include:

  • Selection of Air ambulance including medical team and state of the art medical equipment for emergency medical flights according to the patient medical condition and needs and the flying time involved
COMMERCIAL FLIGHTS:
